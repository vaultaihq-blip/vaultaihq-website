Privacy Policy — Pick&Pay

Last updated: May 2026

Developed by Vault AI HQ · vault.ai.hq@gmail.com

1. Data we collect

  • Name and email (to create an account)
  • Camera and photos (to scan receipts via OCR)
  • Receipt data (items and amounts, for bill splitting)
  • Push notification tokens (for session invites)
  • Credit purchase history (via Google Play / App Store)

2. How we use your data

  • Provide the bill-splitting service
  • Process in-app purchases via RevenueCat
  • Send session notifications
  • Improve the app and prevent abuse

3. Third parties

Receipt images are processed by:

  • OpenAI — OCR analysis — openai.com/privacy
  • Supabase — database and authentication — supabase.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat — purchase management — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Google Firebase — push notifications — firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. Data retention

Account data is kept until you delete your account.

Receipt data is kept for 90 days after a session is closed.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your data.

You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the app: Profile → Delete Account.

6. Children

This service is not intended for children under 13.
